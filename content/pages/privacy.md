# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TechTutorials Pro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information for analytics and improvement purposes:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring website
- Pages viewed and time spent
- Click patterns

### Cookies
We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience:
- **Essential Cookies** - Required for website functionality
- **Analytics Cookies** - Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
- **Advertising Cookies** - Used by third-party advertisers (Google AdSense) to show relevant ads

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
- Improve our content and user experience
- Display relevant advertisements
- Monitor and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdSense
We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our website. You can opt out by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Data Retention

We retain analytics data for 14 months. Personal information is not stored beyond what is necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
